Theorem 6 (Decidability)
Theorem decidability: ∀ E A B,
    WF E A →
    WF E B →
    wf_env E →
    (Sub E A B ∨ ¬ Sub E A B).
Proof with auto.
  intros.
  eapply decidability_aux...
  apply soundness_wf...
  apply soundness_wf...
Qed.
